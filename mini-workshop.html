Having Hard Conversations

The Mini Workshop

If you’re struggling to increase your confidence in having hard conversations or you struggle to manage them once they get going, this hour-long mini workshop is the boost you’ll need. You’ll get tools you can use immediately—and that respect the nuance of your situation— in your next tough conversation.

Whether it’s a chat with your boss about a crucial decision, a teammate who makes offensive jokes, or someone who’s underperforming on a team, this workshop takes the fear out of engaging in that kind of conflict.
